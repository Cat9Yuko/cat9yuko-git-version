This department is mainly responsible for organizing and conducting researches regarding the major relationship and key issues of water resources in respect of national economy and social development.


It is also charged with organizing and conducting the researches in the following fields: mid to long term development strategy of water resources, water resources development in different watersheds or zones, water resources administration system and mechanism, sustainable development of water resources, etc.


This department enjoys a prevailing advantage in the research of the following domains: water resources administration system and operation mechanism, water resources investment and finance, construction of water economy society, flood control and drought fighting, international rivers, etc. In addition, it has expanded gradually its research into the field of insurance, taxation, and planning program, etc.
