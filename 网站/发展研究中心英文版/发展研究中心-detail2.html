About US

The Research and Development Center is an institute directly under the Ministry of Water Resources. To suit the requirements of water resources reform and development, it is formed according to “total emerging and restructuring” principle and by emerging the previous Policy Research Center, Information Research Institute, and the economic research portion of Comprehensive Development and Administration Center of the Ministry of Water Resources. The main responsibilities assigned by the Ministry to this Center are: research and decision consultancy on overall and comprehensive issues in respect of water resources development strategy, policy, economy, laws and regulations, provision of strategic support to water resources reform and development.


The Center has 109 staff and boasts a strong and competent research and consultancy team, a.mong which mass are PhD or master. Out of all the research staff, many have been awarded senior professional qualifications. One group of young and middle-aged researching staff is becoming the backbone in water resources policy research and decision consultancy, among which the excellent ones have become in their own specialty the academic leaders recognized both inside and outside this industry. The Center has established an expert consultancy commission staffed by academicians, famous specialists, and experts with rich experiences in water resources administration or presiding over certain work in water resources. This commission provides consultancy to key research subjects, and helps guarantee that water resources policy research and decision consultancy are authoritative.


This center has the following departments or organizations: Functional Departments, Applied Research Departments and Basic Research Departments. For the past few years, the Center has achieved great research results and the research capability has been developing incessantly. Some important achievements have been put into general application within and outside water resources industry, and have won recognition from appropriate officials, experts and professionals. Social and economic benefits have also been realized in the meantime. As a result, the leading position of this center in water resources soft science research has been established gradually. The publications edited by Water Resources Development Press (an affiliated secondary entity of this center) such as “Report on China Water Resources Development”, “Water Resources Development Research”, “Water Resources and Hydropower Engineering”, and “watersite.com.cn” website command a great influence within and outside this industry. The affiliated Zhongshuiruize Consultancy Corp. Ltd. is devoted to water resources information and hydraulic engineering technology consultancy, and provides effective solutions to water resources reform and development. The affiliated Yuanxin Hydraulic and Electric Technology Development Corp. and Aotengboyu Engineering Consultancy Corp. Ltd. are expanding their business in the field of software and hardware development, network technology service and engineering consultancy.
